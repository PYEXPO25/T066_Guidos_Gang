DESCRIPTION

Delhi Capitals (DC), formerly known as Delhi Daredevils, has evolved into one of the IPL's most promising franchises in recent years. With a strong emphasis on nurturing young talent, DC has featured dynamic players like Rishabh Pant, Shreyas Iyer, and Prithvi Shaw. Under the guidance of legendary coach Ricky Ponting, the team made it to the final in 2020 and was a consistent contender in the playoffs. The franchise represents India's capital and plays its home games at the Arun Jaitley Stadium. With a balanced squad and a hunger for success, DC continues to aim for their maiden IPL title.
