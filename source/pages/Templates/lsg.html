DESCRIPTION

The Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) are a rising force in the Indian Premier League (IPL), representing Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. Established in 2021 and owned by the RPSG Group, LSG plays its home matches at the Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium. Impressively, the team reached the playoffs in both of its first two seasons (2022 & 2023), signaling a strong debut presence. In the 2025 season, Rishabh Pant leads the squad as captain, succeeding KL Rahul, with strategic guidance from head coach Justin Langer. With a powerful mix of explosive hitters like Nicholas Pooran, crafty bowlers like Ravi Bishnoi, and young Indian stars such as Mayank Yadav, Ayush Badoni, and Mohsin Khan, LSG is geared up for a championship run in the near future.
