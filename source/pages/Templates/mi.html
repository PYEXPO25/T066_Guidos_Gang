DESCRIPTION

The Mumbai Indians (MI) are a professional cricket franchise based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, competing in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Established in 2008, they are owned by Indiawin Sports Pvt. Ltd., a subsidiary of Reliance Industries Limited. MI has secured five IPL titles (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020), making them one of the league's most successful teams. As of the 2025 season, the team is captained by Hardik Pandya and coached by Mahela Jayawardene, with home matches held at Mumbai's Wankhede Stadium. The franchise has also expanded into the Women's Premier League (WPL), introducing a women's team in 2025.
