WELCOME TO GUJARAT TITANS

DESCRIPTION

The Gujarat Titans (GT) are a prominent cricket franchise based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, competing in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Established in 2021, the team made a remarkable debut in the 2022 season by clinching the IPL championship title. Owned by Irelia Sports India Private Limited, a subsidiary of CVC Capital Partners, the Titans play their home matches at the Narendra Modi Stadium, the world's largest cricket venue. As of the 2025 season, the team is captained by Shubman Gill and coached by Ashish Nehra. In the 2025 IPL auction, GT made significant acquisitions, including star pacers Kagiso Rabada and Mohammed Siraj, as well as explosive English batsman Jos Buttler, signaling their intent to build a formidable squad.
