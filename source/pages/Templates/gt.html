Gujarat Titans (GT), based in Ahmedabad, is one of the newest and most exciting franchises in the Indian Premier League (IPL), making an immediate impact by winning the title in their debut season in 2022. Playing their home matches at the magnificent Narendra Modi Stadium, GT has quickly built a reputation for resilience, all-round strength, and exceptional leadership. With a mix of global stars and local talent, the team features standout performers like Shubman Gill, Rashid Khan, and Mohit Sharma. Initially captained by Hardik Pandya, the team now eyes a strong comeback in the 2025 season with a re-energized squad and strategic focus.
