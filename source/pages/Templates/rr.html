WELCOME TO RR

DESCRIPTION

The Rajasthan Royals (RR) are a professional cricket franchise based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, competing in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Established in 2008 as one of the original eight IPL teams, they achieved immediate success by winning the inaugural championship under the leadership of Shane Warne. Known for identifying and nurturing emerging talent, RR has a reputation for unearthing high-potential players. As of the 2025 season, the team is captained by Sanju Samson and coached by Rahul Dravid, with home matches held at the Sawai Mansingh Stadium in Jaipur. The current squad features a blend of seasoned internationals and promising domestic players, including Nitish Rana, Shimron Hetmyer, and Yashasvi Jaiswal. Despite facing challenges over the years, RR remains a competitive force in the IPL, aiming to replicate their early success in upcoming seasons.
